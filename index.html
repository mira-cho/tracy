About Tracy

As a Licensed Marriage and Family Therapist, I specialize in somatic therapy, helping clients reconnect with their bodies and develop a deeper understanding of their nervous system responses.

My Journey

With over 10 years of experience, I've dedicated my practice to helping individuals navigate life's challenges through a holistic approach that honors the connection between mind and body.

My Philosophy

I believe that healing happens when we listen to our bodies, recognize our emotions, and develop awareness of our patterns. Through mindfulness and somatic practices, we can create lasting change and resilience.

Education & Training

Master's in Marriage and Family Therapy
Certified in Somatic Experiencing
Trained in Mindfulness-Based Stress Reduction

My Approach

I integrate somatic therapy with mindfulness practices to help you develop a deeper connection with your body and emotions.

  • Understanding the Nervous System

    Learn how your body responds to stress and trauma, and develop tools to regulate your nervous system.

  • Embracing Mindfulness

    Cultivate present-moment awareness to break free from automatic patterns and create new possibilities.

  • Recognizing Emotions

    Develop the capacity to identify, express, and process emotions in a healthy and constructive way.

  • Developing Awareness

    Build a deeper understanding of yourself, your patterns, and your needs to create lasting change.
